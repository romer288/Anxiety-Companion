Install Anxiety Companion on Your Device

Anxiety Companion can be installed as an app on your device without having to visit the app store. This guide will walk you through the installation process for different devices.

For iPhone and iPad Users

1 Open Safari browser and visit the Anxiety Companion website.

This installation method only works in Safari, not other browsers on iOS.

2 Tap the share button at the bottom of the screen (a square with an arrow pointing up).
3 Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen" in the share menu.
4 Customize the name if you wish, then tap "Add" in the top-right corner.
Note: After installation, you'll find the Anxiety Companion icon on your home screen. When opened from there, it will run in full-screen mode without Safari's browser controls.

For Android Users

1 Open Chrome browser and visit the Anxiety Companion website.
2 Tap the menu button (three dots in the top-right corner).
3 Select "Install app" or "Add to Home Screen" from the menu.
4 Follow the prompts to complete the installation.

For Desktop Users (Chrome, Edge, or Safari)

1 Open your browser and visit the Anxiety Companion website.
2 Look for the install icon in the address bar (usually a small plus sign or computer icon).
3 Click the install button and follow the prompts to complete installation.
